Lasagna

For the cheese lover!

The best dish to impress your italian extended family! This dish combines a delicious blend of cheeses with spices that will leave your grandmother thinking back to her days in Italy

This recipt is made to be quick and easy, allowing for any other party preparations you still need to make.

Ingredients

  1. 1 serving cooking spray
  2. ½ pound bulk mild Italian sausage
  3. 3 tablespoons butter
  4. 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  5. 2 cups low-fat milk
  6. 1 pinch salt
  7. 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  8. 1 egg
  9. 1 (15 ounce) container part-skim ricotta cheese
  10. 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  11. 2 (24 ounce) jars marinara sauce, divided
  12. 1 (9 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, divided
  13. 1 cup chopped cremini mushrooms, divided
  14. 1 (16 ounce) package shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese, divided
  15. ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
